How do input the names of my lists on the home page?

To enter the names of your lists on our home page, type out all of your list names separated by commas.

For example, if I want three lists, I'd enter the names of them like this: "[listname1],[listname2],[listname3]".

How do I add a task? Add a list?

To add a task, type what you want to call the task (ex. "study for math test") in the input area next to the button called "Add Task!". Then, click the button!

To add a list, similar to adding a task, type what you want to name the list (ex. "School") in the input area next to the button called "Add List!". Then, click the button!

How do I change the name of a list?

To change the name of a list, type the current or old name of the list, a comma, and then what you want the list's new name to be.

It should look a little something like this: "[old name],[new name]".

How do I delete tasks? Delete lists?

To delete a task, hold the "shift" button down on your keyboard and click on the task you want to delete. If you are having issues, try refreshing the page.

To delete a list, hold the "shift" button down on your keyboard and click on the general area of the list you want to delete. DO NOT click the list's name. If you are having issues, try refreshing the page.

What does the "Clear all Tasks!" button do?

It clears all of the tasks across all lists on the page. Essentially it is a "Delete All!" button, and the changes made by it cannot be undone.

What does the "Save!" button do?

It saves all your work; tasks, lists, and all. We highly reccomend you click it before exiting the page just to be sure everything is saved. However, we do currently have an early version of autosave that does save your work automatically.
